Guess the FFXIV Boss

Score: 2000

How to Play

Guess the name of the FFXIV boss based on the image shown.

You start with 2000 points. Each incorrect guess reduces your points. You can use hints, but they will cost points:

  • Type of Content: -200 pts
  • Name of Content: -300 pts
  • First Letter: -300 pts
  • Patch: -200 pts

If your score reaches 0 or below, the game is over.
